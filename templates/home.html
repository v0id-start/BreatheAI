Welcome to Breathe AI

What is Breathe AI?

Breathe AI is a machine learning solution to identifying and diagnosing cases of community acquired pneumonia (CAP) by utilizing a Convolutional Neural Network to recognize patterns found in common Chest X-Rays.




Breathe AI's Mission

Breathe AI's mission is to provide an accessible, fast, and likely diagnosis of pneumonia. The COVID-19 pandemic has put immense pressure on all healthcare workers. The American Hospital Association projects that by late-January, 2021, 80% of all hospital beds in the US will be occupied, and states including California will be at 120% occupancy by February. In many rural communities, a seasoned doctor consultant is either unavailable or expensive. Breathe AI is a free online tool that works in the web and on mobile to provide insight to a chest x-ray.

About Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection found in the lungs that agitates and inflames alveoli (air pouches), causing them to fill with fluid or pus. This causes symptoms such as a cough, fever, chills and trouble breathing.
